About ZkPolice

ZkPolice is the first local profiling platform for blockchain dev teams and a petition platform for scam victims to find true legal justice.

From the early days of initial coin offerings, the crypto space has been ravaged by a ton of project team scams. From rug pulls, pump and dump scams, staged contract hacks, to project abandonment. Although several measures exist to make the project team accountable, their numbers do not seem to be reducing nonetheless.

With the ZkPolice ecosystems, anyone can create a petition against a team, and with a minimum of 10 supporting signatures, our team will do an open analysis to validate the claim before sending the details to the appropriate authority.

Also, beyond the superficial KYC compliance available, project teams will now be properly profiled, for professional life history, location, criminal records, and every other relevant information that investors should be aware of.

What we offer our community

Petition for project teams with KYC

ZkPolice offers a petition signing Dapp that allows anyone who holds over $50 worth of the ZkPolice native tokens to sign petitions against a scam team. Every petition needs a minimum of 10 supporting signatures to become a petition valid for an investigation.

Region-specific team member profiling

ZkPolice profiling badge uses region-specific profiling services to do a thorough profiling background check and professional history of at least a key-stake holder of every project, with the highest level of confidentiality. These details are only released to the relevant authority in the event that investors collectively sign a fraud petition with substantial evidence.

ZkPolice Profiling badge

This allows investors to know that the ZkPolice team with the help of a relevant profiling service has done thorough profiling and background check of a project team. The profiling process includes- validating the team leads ID, validating the team lead address, validating the team leads professional history, validating the team lead crime history if any, and any relevant details that investors need to know about who is taking their money.
